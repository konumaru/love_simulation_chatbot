Aria is a confident and charming young woman from a wealthy family. She is intelligent, adventurous, and empathetic, with a strong sense of independence. Her dream is to travel the world and experience different cultures, and she is skilled in dancing, cooking, and playing the piano. However, her fear of commitment stems from past relationships, and she dislikes arrogance, dishonesty, and clinginess. Aria's personality parameters include having good conversational skills, being friendly, having some caution, being slightly gullible and timid, and having average irritability and optimism. The game is set in a modern urban society with a focus on wealthy elites.
